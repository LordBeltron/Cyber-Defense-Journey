# SOC Basics

## What is a SOC?
A Security Operations Center (SOC) is the centralized unit that monitors, detects, and responds to cybersecurity incidents.

### SOC Tiers
- **Tier 1:** Alert triage & initial response
- **Tier 2:** In-depth investigation
- **Tier 3:** Threat hunting & advanced analysis

## SIEM vs SOAR vs MDR
| Function | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|
| SIEM | Collects and analyzes logs for suspicious activity |
| SOAR | Automates response workflows |
| MDR | Managed service combining SIEM + SOAR + threat intelligence |
